How to use StockMonk
📊
Dashboard
Shows the top buy signals and most-talked-about stocks on Reddit right now. Click any card to see full details. Scores above 60 are Buy Watch.
🔍
Search
Look up any stock by ticker. Works for US, UK, Indian, and European stocks — try NVDA, TSCO, RELIANCE, SAP, ASML.
📅
Earnings
Upcoming shows earnings in the next 14 days with EPS estimates. Recent shows who beat or missed last week. Red pill = report in ≤2 days.
⭐
Watchlist
Add up to 20 tickers to track live prices and signals. Search a stock then tap + Watch to add it. Tap a card to refresh details.
